Who will take care of America's Grandmothers?

Getting around is the biggest disability for elderly women over the age of 65.

By JoAnna Klein

April 29, 2015

For the elderly, getting around the house is a problem. It's especially a problem for women in the United States. According to the U.S. Census Bureau's 2012 American Community Survey, in every single state in the U.S., including Peurto Rico, the percentage of disabled women with an ambulatory disability outnumbers the percentage of men.
